Why Choose EB-2 NIW Visa?

No Job Offer Required

Unlike other visa categories, EB-2 NIW doesn't require a specific job offer from a U.S. employer.

Self-Petition

You can file your own petition without needing an employer sponsor.

Permanent Residency

Direct path to U.S. permanent residency (green card) for you and your family.
